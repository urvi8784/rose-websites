🌹 Symbolism Behind the Petals 🌹

❤ Red Rose:

Symbolizes deep love, passion, and respect. A timeless emblem of romance and courage.

🤍 White Rose:

Represents purity, innocence, and new beginnings. Often seen at weddings or to express remembrance.

💛 Yellow Rose:

Celebrates friendship, cheerfulness, and joy. Perfect for brightening someone’s day.

💜 Lavender Rose:

Enchantment and love at first sight. It carries a mystical, regal aura.

🖤 Black Rose:

A symbol of farewell, mystery, or rebirth. It evokes depth and transformation.

💙 Blue Rose:

Represents the impossible or unattainable—beautiful in its uniqueness and rarity.

🧡 Orange Rose:

Conveys enthusiasm, desire, and excitement. A vibrant expression of admiration and fascination.

🍑 Peach Rose:

Symbolizes sincerity, gratitude, and modesty. Often given as a gentle thank-you or to mark a closing deal.

💚 Green Rose:

Represents fertility, harmony, and rejuvenation. A symbol of peace and continuous growth.

🌸 Pink Rose:

Denotes grace, admiration, and sweetness. A classic way to show gentle affection and appreciation.

🤎 Cream Rose:

Stands for charm, thoughtfulness, and elegance. Slightly warmer than white, expressing calm admiration.

🍷 Burgundy Rose:

Signifies unconscious beauty. A deeper, mysterious variation of love often associated with devotion and passion.

🧃 Coral Rose:

Blends the meanings of orange and pink—symbolizing desire, excitement, and admiration.

🎨 Salmon Rose:

Associated with enthusiasm, optimism, and positive energy. It radiates a sense of cheerful passion.

🌈 Multicolor Rose:

Embraces unity, joy, and whimsy. Often used to convey mixed emotions or a celebration of diversity.
